About the cooperative

Why families choose Samartha Sahakara Sangham

We are a community-focused group working to bring trustworthy household essentials to local families. Our goal is simple: offer products people can use every day with confidence, while supporting a model built on cooperation, care, and social good.

We focus on quality you can trust, with products chosen for everyday cooking and family use.
When you buy from us, you support a community-driven initiative that values dignity, local participation, and shared growth.
Our aim is to give customers fair pricing, dependable products, and a buying experience that feels personal and honest.
